Obsessive Compulsive Rabbit Assassins
By James Kelly & Eric Marcinowski
-------------------------------------

Rabbits can be jerks in the garden. Do YOU like being a jerk in a garden? Well good news! Obsessive Compulsive Rabbit Assassins features you, a Rabbit Assassin, tasked with eliminating those rabbits who have the audacity to have a clean garden. Do what you must to distract them, then betray them for their betrayal; like a true ninja.
